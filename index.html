Canada's Best Doughnuts

Canada is commonly known to be the country of Maple Syrup or Poutine, but surprisingly, Canadians eat more doughnuts per capita than any other country in the world. It is even widely believed to be the unofficial national dish of Canada, and Canadians even celebrate National Donut Day on the first Friday of June. Canada is home to some incredible doughnut shops that offer unique flavours and creative designs. Whether you are up for something new and creative or just craving a classic glazed doughnut, here are some of the best creative and delicious doughnut stores across Canada that every doughnut lover should try.

Donut Monster

Address: Locke St S, Hamilton, ON L8P 4B9
Instagram: https://www.instagram.com/donutmonsterhamilton/

Donut Monster, located in Hamilton, Ontario, is known for their high-quality ingredients, innovative, and crazy doughnuts. With 45,000+ followers on Instagram, Donut Monster has grown a huge fanbase of doughnut lovers who love this shop's unique flavours and creative designs.

This shop is particularly famous for their crazy non-traditional take on doughnuts, like their:

  • Dill Pickle doughnut (a delicious sweet dill herb glaze and topped with a panko-crusted deep fried pickle spear)
  • Hamilton Hurricane doughnut (consists of citrus, passionfruit, and white rum in the glaze)

They have every flavour that you can imagine, ensuring that every visit brings a new opportunity to try new flavours. Their menu also offers vegan and traditional/classic doughnuts such the Apple Fritter and the vegan Coconut Chocolate donut. Donut Monster's menu is ever-evolving, allowing for a fun and crazy doughnut experience.

Beyond their creative doughnut menu, they also offer delicious coffee, serving a range of espresso based beverages and drip coffees, as well as vegan and traditional milkshakes. The milkshakes have classic flavours like vanilla and chocolate, but they also offer unique seasonal flavours such as the Almond Joy milkshake.

Donut Monster is a great place for doughnuts lovers, as they offer a wide range of different creative doughnut flavours that no other store has ever done. They also offer coffee and milkshakes to enjoy, which complements the fun atmosphere that this store gives. Donut Monster is one of Canada's most innovative and creative doughnut stores, and whether you are up to something new and creative or craving a classic doughnut, Donut Monster is the store for all doughnut lovers to visit and try.

Glory Hole Doughnuts

Address: 1505 Gerrard St E, Toronto, ON M4L 2A4
Instagram: https://www.instagram.com/ghdoughnuts/

With over 40,000 Instagram followers and known for their creativity, high-quality ingredients, and unique flavours, the award-winning Glory Hole Doughnuts is one of Canada's best doughnut stores, offering many creative and different types of doughnuts.

The shop specializes in three distinct styles of doughnuts, including:

  • Cake (rich and dense, making it perfect for glazes, bold, and creative toppings)
  • Ex. The strawberry rhubarb cheesecake doughnut from Glory Hole Doughnuts
  • Yeast-raised (pillowy soft with a light and airy texture, which practically melt in your mouth)
  • Ex. The ferrero rocher doughnut from Glory Hole Doughnuts - dipped in Nutella with feuilletine, roasted hazelnuts, and a chocolate drizzle
  • Vegan (made with a coconut base that delivers just a hint of coconut that compliments other flavours without overpowering them)
  • Ex. The vegan glazed doughnut from Glory Hole Doughnuts

Glory Hole Doughnuts also change their flavours occasionally and seasonally, offering limited time flavours and holiday-themed doughnuts, so there are always new flavours everytime you visit.

For Valentine's Day, Glory Hole Doughnuts introduced three new limited time creative doughnuts:

  • Strawberry jelly-filled doughnuts
  • London Fog (earl grey tea infused pastry cream)
  • Citrus Chai Brûlée (chai tea finished with torched orange zest sugar)

This local Toronto shop continues to be the go-to destination for doughnut lovers looking for something creative, bold, and out of the ordinary. Glory Hole Doughnuts is known to be the trendsetter of everything doughnut related, making it one of the best and influential doughnut stores in Canada and also ensuring that returning customers always have new flavours to try on their next visit.
